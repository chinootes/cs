Bug Life Cycle Stages

  1. New/Open
  2. Deferred/Postponed
  3. Assigned
  4. In Progress
  5. Fixed
  6. Pending Retest
  7. Verified/Closed
  8. Reopened

These stages can vary according to the organization and team.
